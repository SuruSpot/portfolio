About.

I'm a Class 12 student and JEE 2027 aspirant who finds it impossible to just memorize things. Physics, math, systems — I need to know why before I can know how.

That obsession bleeds into everything I build. Custom Arch Linux environments, Java systems, web platforms from scratch. Not to pad a portfolio — because building is thinking made visible.

Thorfinn crossed an ocean to find what he was looking for. Musashi lost everything and came out sharper. Takumi didn't train on a track — he trained on a mountain pass, alone, at 3am, delivering tofu. Elliott saw the systems everyone else ignored. I think about those stories a lot. Going all the way in is the only approach that makes sense to me.
